HONESTY IS THE BEST POLICY

A poor wood-cutter was felling a tree on the bank of a river. By chance his axe fell into it & he began to weep. Mercury, a kind god, happened to pass that way. He asked him what the matter was. When he listened the whole story, he took pity on the poor old man. He jummped into the river, and showed him a golden axe. Is it yours? he asked.No, sir, replied the wood-cutter; mine was not so beautiful.He dived again & brought out a silver axe.Is it yours? he asked again.No, sir replied the honest man. Mine was not so bright. He dived for the third time and brought out the iron axe which had been lost.Oh! yes, that is mine, exclaimed the wood-cutter, as soon as he saw it. Mercury was greatly pleased with the wood-cutter. And gave him all the three axes as a reward for his honesty.

Moral:

we should always be honest in our lives.
